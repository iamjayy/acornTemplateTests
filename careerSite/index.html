Join the world's largest leading stairlift company today

Acorn is the world's biggest business dedicated solely to the manufacture, supply and maintenance of home stairlifts.

Acorn is a global business which employs around 1,500 people worldwide. We're always looking for talented people to join the Acorn team.

Career opportunities are available across all our departments, including manufacturing, engineering and design, stairlift installation and maintenance, sales and marketing, international logistics and many more.

Acorn has held the prestigious Investors in People accreditation continually since 2006. This Government-backed scheme recognizes businesses which commit to the continual training and development of employees and strive to make their company a great place to work.

Acorn has a strong focus on staff retention and progression. New employees complete a thorough induction programme, with further training opportunities enabling them to enhance their skills, improve their personal profiles and progress within the business.

Acorn's Vision and Values

Acorn's vision is quite simply 'to be the best stairlift provider in the world' . Ever since the company was established in 1992, we've been striving to achieve that goal by following seven key principles:

  • Improving the quality of life for our customers who face mobility challenges
  • Investing in our people through training and development
  • Redefining industry standards
  • Employing Service Excellence by going the extra mile for new, existing and internal customers
  • Having a Quality Product
  • Using best practice for manufacturing
  • Employing innovative leading-edge technology

Meeting our customers' needs to the very best of our ability has always been at the core of all that we do at Acorn. To that end, we developed a set of Core Values which every Acorn employee is expected to embrace and put into practice, no matter what role they perform within the company. We even have monthly awards schemes to recognizes and reward employees who best embody and demonstrate Acorn's Core Values in their everyday working lives.
